Welcome to Fantallometrik website

  

Fantallometrik is the international platform to facilitate the calculation of volume, biomass and carbon stocks for forests and trees outside forests. The platform allows accessing and sharing data and tools to
  1. provide access to continually updated databases containing tree allometric equation, conversion factors and wood densities per ecological zones, countries and continents
  2. facilitate access to the most relevant scientific literature
  3. facilitate the selection and comparison of tree allometric equations
  4. provide access to manuals, documentations and tutorials
  5. assess volume, biomass and carbon stocks in different tree compartments and forest plots considering all available allometric equations
  6. provide the statistical information
  7. share data with the fantallometrik community

Allometric equation's database center

  

Download the allometric equations for your project, country and continent!

The Allometric equation's database center provides access to the tree allometric equations by ecological zones, countries or continents. They can be used to assess volume, biomass and carbon of trees in natural forests, outside the forest and in forest plantations. The data are collected from the scientific literature, reports from logging companies, conference proceedings, national reports (access to the references).

The data are harmonized to facilitate their use (access to the methodology). The harmonization procedure takes into account the tree compartments that are considered by the equations (stem, root, branch, leaf, etc..) geographical location (ecological zone, country, continent), the references of the authors and the statistics (correlation coefficient , sample size, confidence intervals etc.). The data are those originally reported by the authors. Errors can appear in certain equations. We try to detect errors and suggest corrections. The users can find a list of suggested equations that we modified.

Fantallometrik software

  

Download the software to assess volume, biomass and carbon stocks.

Fantallometrik software is the first tool for calculating the volume, biomass and carbon that takes into account the existing allometric equations at the continental level. It is a robust tool that allows provide quality control and quality assurance, and comparability of the data. It aims to facilitate and improve the calculations carried out by scientific research, forest companies, NGOs and forestry institutions. It consists of different modules that serve different purposes and are complementary.

  1. Comparing allometric equations and controlling and quality assurance
  2. Calculating volume, biomass and carbon stocks
  3. Inserting new allometric equations
  4. Sharing of data to enrich the existing databases
