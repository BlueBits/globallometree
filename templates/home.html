GlobAllomeTree is the international platform for tree allometric equations to support volume, biomass and carbon stock assessment for trees and forests. The platform allows accessing:

Updated databases containing tree allometric equations, and conversion factors per ecological zones, countries and continents

Updated links to scientific literature related to tree allometric equation and forest biomass assessment

Manuals and tutorials to facilitate assessment of volume, biomass and carbon stocks

Software for comparing tree allometric equations, assess volume, biomass and carbon stocks per tree components and forest types
