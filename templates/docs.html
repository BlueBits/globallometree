1 Scientific litterature

Fantalloemtrik DATABASE (c) and Fantallometrik SOFTWARE are based on the latest scientific researches. This section provides you the links to the most relevant scientific articles on the use of allometric equations for volume, biomass and carbon stocks.

2 Documentation

This section provides reports and documentation on the installation and use of Fantallometrik SOFTWARE (c) and Fantallometrik DATABASE (c). It also provides manuals and literature on the development of allometric equations and assessment of volume, biomass and carbon stocks.

3 Share your experiences

You are welcome to provide comments and suggestions to update the list of the scientific publications and improve the documentation, manuals and reports on Fantallometrik DATABASE and Fantalometrik SOFWTARE. On the forum: share your experiences and questions with the Fantallometrik community.
