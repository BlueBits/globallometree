Manual for building tree volume and biomass allometric equations: from field measurement to prediction.

This manual is mainly dedicated to support the development of tree allometric equations for volume, biomass or carbon stock assessment. It is dedicated to students, engineers or researchers who want to build volume or biomass equations for the purposes of forest management, carbon sequestration or of studying forest ecosystem functioning. The manuals explains the basis for destructive field measurement, laboratory analysis, database management, statistical analysis, model fitting and forest volume, biomass and carbon stocks.
